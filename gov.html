BCBP Governance Team Role

​The Brotherhood of Christian Businessmen and Professionals (BCBP) organizes its leadership through a Governance Team (GT) at each chapter level. While specific roles and titles may vary slightly between chapters, the core structure typically includes the following positions:​

Common Roles in a BCBP Chapter Governance Team

Chapter Head:

Leads the chapter, ensuring alignment with BCBP's vision and mission.​

Mission Director:

Oversees evangelization efforts, including outreach programs and events.​

Formation Director:

internal member development, such as training and spiritual formation.

Program and Services Director:

Coordinates social action initiatives and advocacy programs.​ ​

Family Life Director:

Supports family-oriented activities and member welfare.​ ​

Treasurer:

Handles financial matters, including tithes and budgeting.​ ​

Unit Leaders:

Serve as liaisons between the governance team and smaller member groups, providing pastoral care.​ ​

Communications and Digital Director:

Manages internal and external communications, including digital platforms.​ ​

Secretary:

Maintains records and documentation for the chapter.​ ​
